Corrections & Clarifications

Detroit Free Press Published 5:57 p.m. ET July 1, 2019 | Updated 6:26 p.m. ET July 1, 2019

To report corrections & clarifications, contact:

Phone, 313-222-6600

Please indicate whether you're responding to content online or in the newspaper.

The following corrections & clarifications have been published on stories produced by the Detroit Free Press newsroom:

July 2019

Life: A July 1 story about Dobre Pierogi gave the wrong city for the story. It's located in Shelby Township. https://www.freep.com/story/entertainment/dining/2019/07/01/pierogi-maker-opens-new-store-macomb-township/1587546001/

June 2019

News: A June 30 story from the Associated Press about former Michigan Gov Rick Snyder being named a Harvard fellow erroneously reported the day of the announcement. It was Friday. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/2019/06/30/former-governor-rick-snyder-harvard-university-fellow/1611867001/

Opinion: A June 23 column was corrected to make clear that the typical starting salary for teachers in Michigan is $41,000, compared with an average starting salary of $35,000 for teachers in Arizona, and Albion Public Schools were closed by a vote of the local school board. https://www.freep.com/story/opinion/2019/06/23/michigan-rural-urban-school-closings/1502107001/

News: A June 22 story on the arrest of Sheefy McFly was  updated to clarify that McFly was also arrested on an existing traffic warrant. https://www.freep.com/story/entertainment/arts/2019/06/22/sheefy-mcfly-arrested-detroit-police-street-art/1538157001/

Life: A June 20 story on Smoothie King had incorrect information on a Livonia location opening. The story was scheduled for a July 1 soft opening. https://www.freep.com/story/life/food/2019/06/20/quide-how-score-free-smoothie-national-smoothie-day/1512022001/

Sports: A June 14 story on a Detroit Tigers & Kansas City Royals game gave the wrong score. The Royals won, 7-3.  https://www.freep.com/story/sports/mlb/tigers/2019/06/14/detroit-tigers-matthew-boyd-mlb-trade-rumors/1453394001/

Sports: A June 13 story on a Detroit Tigers & Kansas City Royals game gave the wrong score. The Royals won, 7-3. https://www.freep.com/story/sports/mlb/tigers/2019/06/13/detroit-tigers-kansas-city-royals-score/1452468001/ 

Business: A June 13 story about a security guard strike at Bedrock-owned buildings misidentified buildings in two photos captions. The buildings included were Chase Tower and the Federal Reserve Building. https://www.freep.com/story/money/business/2019/06/12/detroit-security-guards-strike-bedrock/1433430001/

News: A June 10 story about a DIA design competition had an incorrect name for Darin McKeever, president and CEO of William Davidson Foundation, a key fundraiser for the project. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/detroit/2019/06/10/dia-design-competition-midtown/1410601001/

Business: A June 5 story on Lord & Taylor store closures included information on previously closed stores as though the closures were pending. The story has since been removed from the website. https://www.freep.com/story/money/business/2019/06/12/detroit-security-guards-strike-bedrock/1433430001/

Business: A June 3 story on contract auto workers misstated the amount of dues paid by United Auto Workers union members. https://www.freep.com/story/money/cars/general-motors/2019/06/03/gm-ford-layoffs-contract-workers/1206527001/

News: A June 3 story on phones and other electronic devices being seized as part of the Flint water investigation misstated the number of officials whose devices were seized. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/flint-water-crisis/2019/06/03/rick-snyders-phone-flint-water-investigation/1333067001/

May 2019

News: A May 24 story about Detroit businessman Robert Carmack incorrectly stated Eric Sabree's involvement in a lawsuit Carmack filed. Sabree and his office were dismissed from the case. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/detroit/2019/05/24/mayor-mike-duggan-foe-robert-carmack-stand-trial-land-deal/1220066001/

News: A May 23 story about a Holland mansion going to auction misspelled Kyle Schrader's name and was clarified to indicate that deposits for Dogwood Manor are refundable. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/2019/05/23/holland-mansion-michigan/3766648002/

News: A May 22 story about a home used to grow illegal marijuana had the incorrect city for the operation. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/oakland/2019/05/22/royal-oak-home-invasion-marijuana-pot/3767843002/

News: A May 20 story about the Fiat Chrysler Automotive deal for a Jeep plant had an incorrect amount for what Hantz Woodland paid for Detroit land. Hantz Woodlands paid 8.3 cents per square foot for the Detroit land. This story has been corrected.https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/detroit/2019/05/20/speculators-moroun-detroit-land-swap-fca-jobs/3664661002/

Business: A May 9 story on scooters returning to downtown Detroit was corrected to show the correct geographic requirements for scooter deployment beyond downtown and Midtown. https://www.freep.com/story/money/business/2019/05/09/its-scooter-season-again-detroit-but-some-prices-up/1140741001/

Life: A May 9 story on a cannoli giveaway by Facebook was edited to clarify the location of the giveway. https://www.freep.com/story/entertainment/dining/2019/05/09/facebook-cannolis-cantoro-italian-market-plymouth-birthday-stories/1155602001/

News: A May 8 story on new uniforms for Detroit City FC was updated to clarify the brewing history of Stroh's. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/detroit/2019/05/08/detroit-city-fc-uniforms-strohs-beer/1141312001/

Entertainment: A May 8 story on Motor City Comic Con was updated after promoters clarified details on appearances by Mark-Paul Gosselaar and Elizabeth Berkley. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/detroit/2019/05/08/saved-bell-elizabeth-berkley-motor-city-comic-con/1145003001/

News: A May 8 story from the Associated Press about a car crash was updated when a police official corrected a previous statement on the condition of a passenger. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/oakland/2019/05/08/west-bloomfield-traffic-stop-crash/1145298001/

April 2019

Entertainment: An April 23 story about the pasta makers at Andiamo restaurants misspelled Anna Peptitti's last name and misstated how many Andiamo restaurants there are in Michigan. https://www.freep.com/story/entertainment/dining/2019/04/23/andiamo-restaurants-pasta-makers/3510147002/

Entertainment: An April 16 story about an upcoming Grace Jones concert incorrectly stated the date of her last metro Detroit appearance. Appearances in the past 25 years included a February 1998 performance at Clutch Cargo's in Pontiac. https://www.freep.com/story/entertainment/music/brian-mccollum/2019/04/16/grace-jones-masonic-temple/3488735002/

News: An April 14 story on measles was updated to clarify when a person with measles is contagious and to make clear that CallingPost is a reference to a company name. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/2019/04/14/michigan-measles-outbreak-orthodox-jewish-community-oakland-county/3411582002/

March 2019

Opinion: In a March 22 guest column about picking a reputable college counselor, the title of the author was misstated. https://www.freep.com/story/opinion/2019/03/22/picking-a-college-counselor/3237560002/

News: A March 14 story on a police officer's accidental drug overdose resulting from a traffic stop misstated the relative strength of fentanyl and morphine. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/wayne/2019/03/14/michigan-state-police-trooper-overdose/3160166002/

Business: A March 9 story on Chrysler's attempts to build a space shuttle gave the wrong location for a plant in Sterling Heights. https://www.freep.com/story/money/business/2019/03/09/chrysler-tried-build-better-space-shuttle/3097167002/

Business: In the March 9 Michigan House Envy there was an incorrect name spelling and place of employment for one of the subjects. Kay Pentzien is a teacher at Claugue Middle School in Ann Arbor. https://www.freep.com/story/money/real-estate/michigan-house-envy/2019/03/09/ann-arbor-apartment-gallery-across-zingermans-sale-1-5-m/3033785002/

News: A March 1 story on a heroin bust was updated to correct the indicator on the PlayStation that led police to the drug bust.  https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/detroit/2019/03/01/fentanyl-heroin-novi-playstation/3021811002/

February 2019

News: A Feb. 21 story about a shooting incident at a wedding contained details from the Wayne County Prosecutor's Office which were inaccurate. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/detroit/2019/02/21/detroit-cop-shooting-wedding-reception/2946324002/

News: A Feb. 15 story on pet coke storage by Marathon Petroleum was revised to clarify the location of Marathon Petroleum's pet coke storage, and add additional background on pet coke piles that were previously stored on the Detroit River's banks. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/wayne/2019/02/15/marathon-pet-coke-petroleum-detroit-river-fugitive-dust/2881924002/

News: A Feb. 15 story on finding John Dingell's grave in Arlington National Cemetery had incorrect details about his burial ceremony, which were removed. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/2019/02/15/john-dingell-grave-arlington-cemetery/2880542002/

Entertainment: A Feb. 8 story on Ima incorrectly identified the style of pork belly common in ramen.  https://www.freep.com/story/entertainment/dining/restaurant-of-the-year/2019/02/08/ima-detroit-free-press-restaurant-year-corktown-madison-heights/2649207002/

News: A Feb. 1 story on LGBTQ rights protections was corrected to indicate that Attorney General Dana Nessel is open to reevaluating the decision of her predecessor, Bill Schuette, in regard to the Elliott-Larsen Civil Rights Act.  https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/2019/02/01/lgbtq-michigan-civil-rights-law/2743034002/

News: A Feb. 1 story on a medical marijuana dispensary in Oakland County misstated the election results for the 2008 proposal to legalize medical marijuana in Michigan. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/oakland/2019/02/01/oakland-county-first-licensed-marijuana-dispensary/2728146002/

January 2019

News: A Jan. 29 story on allergies to the cold misspelled the word urticaria. https://www.freep.com/story/news/local/michigan/2019/01/29/cold-uticaria-allergy-symptoms/2708444002/

Opinion: A Jan. 26 letters to the editor column had the wrong city of residence for Rhonda Wendell. https://www.freep.com/story/opinion/readers/2019/01/26/letters-trump-should-apologize-government-shutdown/2675312002/
